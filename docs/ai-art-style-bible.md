# Black Halo AI Art Style Bible

## Core thesis

The world should feel like holy grandeur collapsing into ash, iron, and stained light. The player reads as a broken saint; the kingdom reads as immaculate violence.

## Visual direction

- Genre: gothic dark fantasy action platformer
- Camera: side-view 2D gameplay art, readable silhouette, premium illustration finish
- Mood: severe, tragic, devotional, predatory
- Palette anchors:
  - ash charcoal `#1b1a20`
  - cathedral stone `#3b3742`
  - tarnished gold `#9a8350`
  - blood oath crimson `#7f1d2b`
  - sacred cyan `#7ad7e0`
  - halo ivory `#e3dbc7`

## Character rules

- The fallen hero must always read as asymmetrical and damaged.
- The paladin order must always read as geometric, ordered, and ceremonial.
- Seraph Vale must combine immaculate authority with intimate menace.
- Every character render should prioritize silhouette clarity over surface detail.

## Environment rules

- Ashfall Bastion: ruined fortifications, torn banners, cracked stone, iron braces
- Reliquary Shaft: chained altars, relic niches, vertical sanctum space
- Mirror Chapel: polished sacred floor, radiant fractures, divine symmetry broken by corruption

## UI rules

- Core HUD must remain clean and highly readable.
- Decorative liturgical framing belongs around the HUD, not inside the gameplay-critical information.
- Text should be rendered as engine text, not baked into generated images.

## Negative prompt guardrails

- no frontal character portraits for gameplay sprites
- no chibi proportions
- no modern clothing
- no firearms
- no bright cartoon saturation
- no wide-angle perspective distortion
- no floating unreadable text
- no soft toy proportions

## Layering standard for gameplay characters

Every final selection should be broken into:

- head
- torso
- upper arm
- lower arm
- back leg
- front leg
- cape or cloth
- weapon
- aura or holy/corruption effect

## Quality checklist

- silhouette readable at a glance
- palette consistent with style bible
- edges clean enough for transparent background export
- front/back layering obvious for cutout animation
- weapon direction readable in profile
- no stray glow or detached fragments
